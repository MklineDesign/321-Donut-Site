The Whatnut

Cuz if you don't know - here we go. The Whatnut was born in a row house on the wrong side of the tracks. It's parents were a flaky, buttery croissant and an ooey-gooey bavarian creme donut. Middle school was hard for a hybrid like Whatnut. Never donut enough for the donuts, nor croissant enough for the croissants. Whatnut's family sensed that it needed a change, so they sent it to Rockaway Beach to live with family. Eventually, Whatnut found it's groove, opened a bakery and served up the good to the weird people of Queens. The rest is history.
